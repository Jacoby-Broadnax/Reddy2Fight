How Your Helping
Please know that your contributions are going directly towards the creation and delivery of care packages that will put smiles on the beautiful faces of many children fighting childhood cancer and other life threatening illnesses.

Our Goal

Our goal is to help these children forget, even if just for a few moments, what they are enduring, and to brings smiles to their faces. Most of these children spend hours, days, weeks, and even months in a hospital room, some in isolation, and many families are searching for ways to keep their children comfortable and entertained. Our care packages are filled with engaging, quality toys, ranging from arts & crafts, to games, to puzzles and more.

Meet Liam Reddy


I have been asked “Why? Why are you doing this?" Without hesitation, I say the two words that inspired me – "LIAM REDDY."

In October 2013, I learned through a Facebook friend about Liam Reddy, a 2-year-old boy in Florida who was fighting a rare form of cancer. Though I had never met Liam or his family, I felt inspired to join the fundraising efforts that the Reddy family's friends were making to help them.

Once the fundraising efforts for Liam had ended, I found myself thinking about him all the time, and wanting to keep supporting and encouraging him through his treatment and recovery.
